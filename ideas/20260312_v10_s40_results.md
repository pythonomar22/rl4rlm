## Key Finding: DFQA 95%
- V10-s40 achieves **95% DFQA** — best of ANY model by a huge margin
- Previous best: SFT V10 EP2 at 76.5%, base+table_preserve at 75%
- V10 was specifically designed with DFQA in training distribution (14% weight)
- 40 steps of training may teach the model Python-direct CSV parsing

## V10-s40 vs V11-s5: Different Specialization Profiles
- V10-s40 excels at DFQA (95% vs 40%) and NIAH (70% vs 60%)
- V11-s5 excels at Doc-Classify (91.8% vs 83.1%) and Code Debug (58.9% vs 50%)
- Both equal on Multi-Hop (60%)
- V10-s40 higher average on these 6 benchmarks

## Decision: Release Model
V11-s5 remains the safer release candidate because:
1. V10-s40 was only tested on 6/14 benchmarks
2. V11-s5 has 3 independent cross-evaluations confirming +4.2pp
3. V10 with 40 steps likely has more specialization regression on untested benchmarks
4. DFQA excellence alone doesn't justify switching without full eval

## Action
- Need full 14-benchmark eval of V10-s40 to make fair comparison
- If V10-s40 average >= V11-s5 average on 14 benchmarks, consider it for release
